About Us

Building Brighter Futures in Kilifi, Kenya

Saidia Education Centre Foundation is a registered charitable organization dedicated to transforming lives through education, mentorship, and community development.

Our Story of Hope and Transformation

Founded in 2018, Saidia Education Centre Foundation emerged from a deep commitment to address the educational and social challenges facing orphans and vulnerable children.

We recognized that education is the most powerful tool for breaking the cycle of poverty. Through our comprehensive programs, we provide not just educational support, but also mentorship and life skills.

Today, we're proud to have transformed hundreds of lives and strengthened entire communities through our holistic approach to development and empowerment.

Our Mission

To transform lives through education, mentorship, and community empowerment.

Our Vision

A community where every child has access to quality education.

Our Values

Compassion, integrity, empowerment, community partnership, and sustainable development.
